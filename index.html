Who is Cristiano Ronaldo?

Cristiano Ronaldo, is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward. He plays for the Premier League club Manchester United and is the captain of the Portuguese national team[1].

He is considered to be one of the greatest footballers of all time, and, by some, as the greatest ever!
